Single Number III

http://www.lintcode.com/en/problem/single-number-iii/
The idea behind this problem is the key to solve it. Just to reduce it to two of the Single Number problem. But it is not easy to see the clue. The key is that for these two numbers, they differ at least in one bit. Find this bit and split all numbers into two groups, one with that bit 1 and one with that bit 0. The rest is very easy.
